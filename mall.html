CITY CENTRE MALL




City Center Doha provides a wide variety of famous international, regional, and local retail brands and services, spread across the five floors. It also features a range of family-oriented offerings which include a 14-screen cinema complex, more than 38 restaurants and a family entertainment Center. Visitors also have the chance to witness a range of spectacular functions, variety of shows, and seasonal events and activities targeting different age groups.Located in the premium business district of Doha, surrounded with Ministries, 5-star hotels, office buildings, and luxury residential towers, City Center Doha shopping mall offers retailers and customers an exceptional space and shopping experience in the heart of Doha. With a proven attraction capacity, from the neighboring districts, and all of Doha, as well as tourists, “City Center Doha” is one of most frequented malls in the city.
